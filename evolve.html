FAQS

Who can join IFXEvolve

Registration is free and anyone can register. However our program is aimed at proficient traders that can demonstrate their skill in accordance to pre-set attributes.

Does every trader automatically qualify for financial backing?

No. Traders are monitored for the duration of the program and need to satisfy the qualifying conditions. Evolve is a performance based program/initiative and various factors are taken into account to consider funding a trader.

Can I register by using my demo account?

No. only live trading accounts that meet all criteria can qualify for the IFX Evolve program/initiative.

Can I use my existing MT4 account held with another broker to register?

No. Only IFX Live accounts qualify for Evolve.

Are there any trading restrictions that apply?

No there are no minimum or maximum volume requirements. The selection committee’s objective is to recruit traders who have proven consistency and longevity.

How long does it take for a trader to qualify for funding?

Traders can receive funding after a minimum of 6 months.

How long will it take to become a professional trader?

Evolve is developed to assist gifted traders advancing their trading careers at their own pace, and no time frame is set.

Why is an IFX Live account the only acceptable account?

Funding is provided by IFXBrokers and it is understandable that for this purpose, that all execution go through its own brokerage.

What is the minimum amount I need to fund my account with?

IFXEvolve require a minimum account size of USD500

Why do I need to fund a portion of the trading account?

Because of the significant amounts of funding potentially available and the associated risk, there is a fundamental belief that a trader has to have “skin in the game” to prevent the mental accounting associated with excessive risk taking when trading free money.

How much funding can I expect to receive?

Traders who satisfy the IFXEvolve criteria and progress through the various phases can receive up to ZAR1,500,000 of cash to trade.

How much can I expect to earn in the Evolve program/initiative

Earnings are uncapped and you will earn 50% commission off all fresh profits generated above the highwater mark.

Can I withdraw my funds?

Naturally any funding provided by IFXEvolve cannot be directly withdrawn, however your profit earnings are conveniently credited to your IFX Wallet monthly where you are free to withdraw at anytime

Will I be compensated for any losses?

No. Each trader is fully responsible for any losses incurred to personal funds. Any losses incurred on trades executed using IFXEvolve funds are the carried by IFXEvolve.

Does IFXEvolve provide training?

No. The IFXEvolve program is aimed at talented traders who already possess trading knowledge and can demonstrate the skill and attitude to become professional traders. For those traders idendtified IFXEvolve is able to provide funding, performance analytics, mentoring, networking opportunities and assistance with industry licensing, in accordance with the IFXEvolve Terms & Conditions.

Can I leave the IFXEvolve program?

Yes, you are free to stop trading or leave the IFXEvolve program at any time.

Can I be removed from the IFXEvolve program?

Yes, the IFXEvolve committee reserves the right to remove traders from the program if they incur significant losses using IFXEvolve funds or are deemed to pose undue risk to IFXEvolve capital.
